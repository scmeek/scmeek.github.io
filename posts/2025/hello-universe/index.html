Novos

Why 'novos?' I've always liked creating new words for new concepts. 'Novos' is the combination of 'nova,' as in 'supernova' or 'hypernova,' and '-os,' as in 'ethos' and 'logos.' The notion is an energetic expansion of knowledge and reason, guided by curiosity and exploration. Maybe a bit silly but representative of where I see this project going.
